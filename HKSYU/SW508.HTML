Levinson's Adult Development Theory

Core Theoretical Premises
核心理論前提

  • Universal stage model of life development人生發展階段具普遍性
  • Specific developmental tasks in each stage每個階段均有特定發展任務
  • Emphasis on adult development着重成年期發展
  • Interaction between individual and social environment個人與社會環境的互動關係
